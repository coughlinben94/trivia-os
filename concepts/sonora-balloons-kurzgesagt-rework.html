7. Distinct at thumbnail[eyes] Ben's call — flatter color-blocking may read as more generic at a glance since the multi-stop painterly dusk sky is what currently makes this theme instantly recognizable. Worth checking side-by-side at real thumbnail size.
What actually changed: envelope rendering only — flat 2-band fill (accent + highlight cap) replacing the 6/4/4-color gore-stripe bands, drop-shadow shading removed, thin rigging lines instead of a solid basket-strap block. Sky flattened from an 11-stop naturalistic gradient to 5 bolder stops. Stars became larger glowing accent dots with a soft box-shadow instead of tiny near-white sparkles — closer to the skill's "glowing particle stream" language.

What did NOT change: anchor count/position, drift paths, sway timing, water band, haze layers, wave count — all reused as-is from the shipped component so this stays a scoped visual variant, not a rebuild.

Real trade-off to flag: the shipped theme's rainbow/sunset/ocean/berry/forest palettes are a documented sanctioned exception (off-family, same treatment as Christmas Eve's garland green) specifically because 5 identical-hue balloons would be visually flat and hard to tell apart mid-drift. Going full single-hue-per-balloon Kurzgesagt-style removes that variety. This mock keeps 3 of the 5 balloons on distinct hues as a middle ground — worth your eyes-check on whether that's still "5 balloons" or starts reading as "3 colors, twice each."
